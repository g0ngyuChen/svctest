YingMusic-SVC

Real-World Robust Zero-Shot Singing Voice Conversion

Overview

YingMusic-SVC is a real-world robust zero-shot singing voice conversion system, featuring Band-Roformer separation, RVC-based timbre shifting, F0-aware timbre adaptation, energy-balanced flow matching, and Flow-GRPO reinforcement learning.
